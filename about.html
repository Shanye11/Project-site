About Me

Hello, my name is Shane Torres, and I am the creator of this website, where I teach animation. My journey began in 2021 when I decided to explore new hobbies and taught myself how to create animations. Since then, my YouTube channel has grown to 17k subscribers, and I now earn over $200 per month from posting my animations. While there’s significant potential to earn more by sharing animations regularly, I currently post infrequently. Through my lessons, I can help you develop your animation skills and guide you toward monetizing your own creations.

What We Do!

Chron YT Tutorials is dedicated to aspiring 2D animation illustrators. Our goal is to offer comprehensive Moho 2D classes and tutorials, closely linked to our YouTube channel. We're passionate about fostering creativity and helping individuals explore their artistic potential through animation. Join us on this exciting journey and bring your imagination to life!
